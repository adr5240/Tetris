Welcome to Falling Blocks! The rules of the game are simple. Seven different types of blocks will fall from the top of the screen. Your job is to clear away as many lines as you can by filling up an entire row. Be careful as the blocks will start to fall faster as you go. Good Luck!

Controls:

⇦ ⇨ to move left and right

⇧ to rotate the block 90

⇩ to drop the block faster

ESC to play/pause (or use the button above the 'next piece' box)
